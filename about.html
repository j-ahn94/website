Jason Ahn is an aspiring software engineer motivated by analytical-talent, curiosity and driven attitude. Born in South Korea and raised in British Columbia, Canada, Jason currently resides in Vancouver.

Holding a Bachelor's degree in Criminology from Simon Fraser University, Jason has worked at three federal government agencies: National Defence [Ottawa], Royal Canadian Mounted Police and Canada Revenue Agency. During his free time, he volunteers at the Vancouver Community Network as a Tech Support II/Programmer. His focus in life is to consistently learn what you love doing.

Jason's interest towards computer and programming first sparked when he first worked at the National Defence while working as an IT Policy Analyst. Being a self-taught programmer, Jason believes that education in STEM program is essential to pioneering the future of tomorrow.

Photos are taken with Ricoh GR II. Work taken with film cameras (Canon AE-1 & Pentax Espio 738) will be uploaded after development. As he loves simplicity and aesthetics, all of the photos are uploaded with no crops/filters.
