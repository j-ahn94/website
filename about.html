Jason Ahn is an aspiring software engineer motivated by analytical-talent, curiosity and driven attitude. Born in South Korea and raised in British Columbia, Canada, Jason currently resides in Vancouver.

Holding a Bachelor's degree in Criminology from Simon Fraser University, Jason has worked at three federal government agencies: National Defence [Ottawa], Royal Canadian Mounted Police and Canada Revenue Agency. His focus in life is to consistently grow and learn what you love doing.

Jason's interest sparked towards computer and programming when he first worked at the National Defence as an Information Management policy analyst. Recognizing that a computer can do something impossible allowed Jason to enter Computer Systems Technology program at British Columbia Institute of Technology. Being a self-taught programmer, Jason believes that education in STEM programs is essential to living in the future world.

During his free time, Jason loves to take pictures with Ricoh GR II, Canon AE-1, Pentax Espio 738 and Canon Rebel XS. As he loves simplicity, all of the pictures taken has no filters..
